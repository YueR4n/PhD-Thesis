# Tourist Group Questionnaire Logic

Here are the overall how and in what logic the online questionnaire is organised.

## General

| Category | Question |
|---|---|
| Personal info | Age group, gender, origin, etc. |
| Frequency | How often do you visit Hong Kong? |
| Purpose | What is the purpose of your visit? |
| Overall feelings | How do you feel about the overall environment / soundscape satisfaction / acoustic comfort? |

## Sundays

| Category | Question |
|---|---|
| Observation | Have you ever seen foreign domestic helpers gathering? |
| Feelings | How did you feel about it? |

## 【360° Panoramic Video Viewing】

Weekday, Saturday, Sunday × 2 sites × 3 locations

### Questionnaire

- Soundscape assessment
- Sound sources

### Interview

| Category | Question |
|---|---|
| Overall impression | What is your overall impression? Would you want to learn and know more about foreign domestic helpers? |
| Soundscape created | When you see the helpers' activities and behavior—what kind of soundscape do you think they create? |
| Noise disturbance | Do you think they face any noise disturbance? |
| Impact on tourism | Do you think the sounds brought by these gatherings affect Hong Kong's tourism? What kind of impact? (positive or negative) |
| Suggestions | Any suggestions / changes in your travel interest? |
