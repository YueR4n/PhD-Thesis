# PaperD

Here are the focus group questions provided. Note that the first author's natural language is Mandarin, and the focus groups were conducted in Cantonese with the help of LAM Lok Him, the third author of the paper. The English translated version is provided in parallel.

### 問卷部分 / Questionnaire Section

#### General

| 廣東話 (Cantonese) | English |
| --- | --- |
| 個人資料：年齡、性別、學歷、職業、喺香港邊區住？ | Personal info: age, gender, education, occupation, which district you live in Hong Kong? |
| 你（或者屋企）有冇請過外傭？相處同感受點？ | Have you (or your family) employed an FDH? How was the relationship and how did you feel? |
| 你嘅工作有冇關於移工或者外傭嘅嘢？做啲乜？有冇遇到啲困難？ | Does your work involve migrants or FDHs? What do you do? Any difficulties you've encountered? |

### Discussion

#### 香港嘅聲音環境 / Hong Kong's Sound Environment

| 廣東話 (Cantonese) | English |
| --- | --- |
| 你覺得香港嘅聲音環境係點㗎？唔同區嘅聲景有咩唔同？ | What do you think Hong Kong's sound environment is like? How do the soundscapes differ across districts? |
| 邊啲聲令你覺得舒服，邊啲令你唔舒服？你嘅感受點（愉悅、有活力、嘈、定係多元）？ | Which sounds make you feel pleasant or uncomfortable? How do you feel (pleasant, vibrant, noisy, diverse)? |
| 有冇啲同聲音有關、印象深刻嘅回憶或者故事（個人、屋企、地方、年代）？ | Any memorable sound-related memories or stories (personal, family, place, era)? |

#### 外傭群體 / Foreign Domestic Helper Group

| 廣東話 (Cantonese) | English |
| --- | --- |
| 喺你心目中，外傭係一個點樣嘅群體？你對佢哋印象點？ | What is your picture of the FDH group? What is your impression of them? |
| 你覺得外傭嘅生活現狀同困境係點（文化、社會認同、空間、政治）？ | What do you see as FDHs' current situation and difficulties (culture, social recognition, space, politics)? |
| 你身邊嘅人（屋企人、朋友、同事）對外傭嘅了解、睇法或者態度係點？ | How do people around you (family, friends, colleagues) understand or view FDHs? |

#### 外傭同聲音環境 / FDHs and the Sound Environment

| 廣東話 (Cantonese) | English |
| --- | --- |
| 你喺週末或者公眾假期有冇見過外傭聚集嘅場面？ | Have you seen FDHs gathering on weekends or public holidays? |
| 外傭嘅活動同行為——你覺得佢哋整咗一個點樣嘅聲景？你點睇、感受點？會唔會覺得影響到你嘅活動？ | Regarding their activities and behavior—what soundscape do you think they create? How do you view and feel about it? Does it affect your activities? |
| 你覺唔覺得外傭帶嚟嘅聲景係一種文化交流嘅展現？ | Do you think the soundscape they bring is an expression of cultural exchange? |
| 你覺得外傭聚集帶嚟嘅聲音，會唔會影響香港旅遊業？有咩影響（好壞都得）？ | Do you think the sounds from FDH gatherings affect Hong Kong's tourism? What impact (positive or negative)? |
| 你覺得呢啲聲音會唔會影響香港居民平日或者週日嘅生活？有咩影響（好壞都得）？ | Do you think these sounds affect residents' weekday or Sunday daily life? What impact (positive or negative)? |

#### 對未來嘅建議 / Suggestions for the Future

| 廣東話 (Cantonese) | English |
| --- | --- |
| 你覺唔覺得佢哋自己都受到噪音困擾？ | Do you think they themselves face noise disturbance? |
| 從聲音嘅角度，你覺得點先可以令更多人睇到同了解外傭嘅現況？ | From a sound perspective, how can more people be made to see and understand FDHs' situation? |
| 你覺得喺公共空間應該點樣平衡唔同群體嘅需要？ | How should the needs of different groups be balanced in public spaces? |
| 你覺得香港可以點改善聲音環境嘅管理？ | How do you think Hong Kong can improve sound environment management? |
| 對於外傭聚集，你覺得使唔使有多啲支援，例如專門嘅活動空間？ | Regarding FDH gatherings, do you think more support is needed, such as dedicated activity spaces? |
