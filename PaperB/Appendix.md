# Appendix

Here are the questions for interviews with FDHs, locals, and cleaners.

## General (all groups)

| Category | Question |
|---|---|
| Personal info | Age group, gender, origin, and how long here? |
| Frequency, timing, and purpose of visit? | Frequency, timing, and purpose of visit? |
| Overall feelings | How do you feel about the overall environment? |
| Soundscape / acoustic comfort | How do you feel about the soundscape / acoustic comfort here? |

## Foreign Domestic Helpers

| Category | Question |
|---|---|
| Activity preference | What activities do you do here, and which do you prefer? |
| Music preference | What kind of music do you listen to or play here? |
| Views on noise | What are your views on the surrounding noise / soundscape? |
| Noise disturbance | Do you experience any noise that bothers you? |

## Gathering Phenomenon

| Category | Question |
|---|---|
| Comparison | How does it differ from ordinary days? |
| Feelings / views | What are your feelings, views, or suggestions about it? |
